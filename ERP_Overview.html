What is this system
One System for the Entire Project

Orchid ERP replaces spreadsheets, WhatsApp chats, and paper registers. Every material movement — from indent request to delivery at site — is tracked digitally with a complete audit trail.

Core Modules
📦
Master Data

One-time setup of your projects, sites, materials, categories, and suppliers.

  • Add unlimited projects & sites
  • Material catalogue with units & HSN codes
  • Supplier directory with GST & contacts
  • Material categories for easy grouping
📝
Indent / Material Requisition

Site Engineer raises a material request. Goes for approval before any purchase.

  • Engineer raises indent with required date
  • Approval by Store Manager / Admin
  • Auto-generates indent number (IND-YYMM-XXXX)
  • Converts directly to Purchase Order
🛒
Purchase Orders

Full procurement workflow from draft to material received at site.

  • Create PO from approved indent
  • Upload 3 supplier quotations
  • Management approval before order
  • Track delivery & mark received
🏪
Stock / Inventory

Real-time stock levels. Every movement is recorded — nothing is manually edited.

  • Live stock per material, project & site
  • Full movement ledger (who moved what, when)
  • Low stock & out-of-stock alerts
  • Opening stock entry for existing stock
🚚
Issues & Transfers

Track material issued to work or moved between sites.

  • Issue material to a site or work area
  • Transfer material from one site to another
  • Approval workflow for issues
  • Stock auto-adjusts on approval
📊
Reports & Dashboard

Ready reports to monitor stock health, purchases, and consumption.

  • Dashboard with key numbers at a glance
  • Current stock report (filter by project/site)
  • Purchase history & supplier analysis
  • Monthly consumption & low stock alerts

Stock & Inventory Module
How Stock Works
Stock is never stored as a number that someone can change. Instead, every transaction (purchase received, material issued, transfer) creates a ledger entry. The current stock is always calculated from these entries — giving a 100% accurate audit trail.
📈
Stock IN (increases stock)
  • Purchase received at site
  • Material returned from site
  • Transfer received from another site
  • Opening stock entry (one-time setup)
📉
Stock OUT (reduces stock)
  • Material issued for work
  • Damage / wastage recorded
  • Transfer sent to another site
Stock Alerts
  • Low Stock: Material falls below minimum quantity set for it
  • Out of Stock: Quantity reaches zero
  • Alert Banner: Warning shown on stock page with count
  • Low Stock Report: Dedicated report listing all low items with shortage
Opening Stock Entry

When you start using the system, you already have materials on hand. The Opening Stock Entry page (Admin only) lets you enter the current quantity of each material once, at project or site level. After this, all future movements are tracked automatically.

Notifications
Automatic Alerts

The system generates in-app notifications so users know when action is needed — no manual follow-up required.

  • Indent submitted — notify approver
  • Indent approved or rejected — notify requester
  • Purchase submitted for approval — notify Admin
  • Purchase approved or rejected — notify creator
  • Material received — stock team notified
  • Low stock level crossed — alert to Store Manager

  • Each user has their own login with password
